Understanding Loan Approval

Credit score and debt-to-income ratio are key factors in loan approval:

  • Credit Score: Range: 300–850. Higher score = better creditworthiness.
  • Credit Score Impact: Higher score → better chances of approval.
  • Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio: DTI = (Monthly Debt / Monthly Income) × 100
  • DTI Impact: Lower DTI → better approval chances. Higher DTI → higher risk.
  • Ideal DTI: Below 36% is favorable.
  • Interest Rates: Lower credit score/DTI → higher interest rates.
  • Improving Profile: Pay off debt, reduce obligations, improve payment history.
  • Age: Older → More financial stability; Younger → Build credit history early.
